About me

Hi! My name is Myles Barney and I am excited that you are here! I am a student at BYU studying computer science and global business. My dream is to leave a lasting impact on the world through innovative new technology and software. My current project is OneGlobe, a social media platform focused on creating meanginful interactions between people from different cultures, different countries, and different mindsets, all over the world!

I had the opportunity to live in Taiwan for two years as a missionary for my church. During that time I grew to love the people of Taiwan and made many lasting friendships that continue to make a difference in my life. I hope that through OneGlobe others will have the same opportunity to reach out and discover the complex lives of people all over the world!

Only by knowing others can we truly come to know ourselves.
